Afghanistan

Aspect Ratio: 2:3
Adopted: August 19th, 2013
the flag of Afghanistan features three equal vertical stripes of black, red, and green color, with the national emblem in white at the center. The black stripe represents the dark past of Afghanistan, the red stripe represents the blood shed for independence and the green stripe represents hope for a bright future. The national emblem at the center consists of a mosque with pulpit and flags on either side, with a national flag over the mosque, and beneath the mosque are Eastern Arabic numerals for the solar year 1298 (1919 in the Gregorian calendar, the year of Afghan independence from the UK).
